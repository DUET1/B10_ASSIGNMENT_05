Donate for Flood Relief in Feni,Bangladesh

The recent floods in Feni and surrounding areas, which began in late August 2024, have caused widespread devastation. Over 90% of the population in districts like Feni and Noakhali has been severely affected, with many homes destroyed and entire communities stranded without basic necessities like clean water and food. Floodwaters have disrupted sanitation systems, leading to a rise in waterborne diseases such as diarrhea and cholera. Roads and infrastructure have also been severely damaged, leaving many areas inaccessible and millions without electricity.
